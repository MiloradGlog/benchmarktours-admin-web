## About the App

The Japan Lean Experience app is designed to enhance your learning journey during lean manufacturing tours and educational experiences. The app provides tools for note-taking, team collaboration, discussions, and reflection to help you capture and retain knowledge from your experiences.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I set up my account?**
A: Your administrator will create an account for you and provide a setup code. On first launch, tap "First time? Setup your account" and enter your email, setup code, and choose a password.

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: When typing in a wrong password you will be prompted to send a reset password request. Our administrators will send you the reset code within 2 days.

**Q: How do I join a tour?**
A: Your administrator will assign you to tours. Once assigned, you'll see available tours on your Tours screen. You must be enrolled in Makoto programs to be assigned.

### Using the App

**Q: How do I take notes during a tour?**
A: During an active tour activity, use the Quick Note feature to capture your observations, insights, and learnings. Note that notetaking is only available while the activity is in progress.

**Q: Can I share photos with my team?**
A: Yes! In the Discussion screen for each activity, you can upload photos that your entire team can view and discuss. You can also take photo notes and then make them public.

**Q: How do team discussions work?**
A: Each tour activity has a dedicated discussion area where you can answer survey questions if survey attached and share observations and upload photos of your teams' discussion whiteboard.

**Q: Can I make notes public?**
A: Yes, when creating or editing a note, you can click the "private" button and be prompted to make it public. Public means other members of the tour can see it. Default is private.

**Q: How do I access past tours?**
A: All your tours (current and completed) are available on the Tours screen. You can review notes, discussions, and uploaded content from any past tour.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is not loading or running slowly**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and restart the app
3. Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
4. Restart your device
5. If issues persist, contact support

**Q: I can't upload photos**
A: Make sure:
1. The app has permission to access your camera and photo library (check device Settings > Japan Lean Experience > Permissions)
2. You have a stable internet connection
3. You have sufficient storage space on your device

**Q: My notes aren't syncing**
A: Notes sync automatically when you have an internet connection. If you're experiencing sync issues:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh on the Tours or Notes screen
3. Ensure you're logged in (check Settings)
4. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: The app crashes or freezes**
A: Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest app version
4. Uninstall and reinstall the app (note: this may require re-login)
5. Contact support with details about when the crash occurs

### Account & Privacy

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password and choose a new one.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your notes, discussions, and data will be permanently removed.

**Q: What data does the app collect?**
A: Please review our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy) for detailed information about data collection and usage.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we take security seriously. All data is transmitted using secure encryption, and we follow industry best practices for data protection. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

### Features

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: No, currently app is online only. In the future we plan to make it local first

**Q: Can I export my notes?**
A: Currently, notes are accessible within the app. For export options, please contact your administrator or our support team.

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Login Issues

**Problem**: "Account Setup Required" message
**Solution**: Your account hasn't been set up yet. Use the setup code provided by your administrator to complete account setup.

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error
**Solution**: Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you've forgotten your password, request a reset from your administrator.

**Problem**: "Network error" during login
**Solution**: Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, the server may be temporarily unavailable.

### Performance Issues

**Slow loading times**:
- Check your internet speed
- Close background apps to free up memory
- Clear app cache (if available in your device settings)

**App using too much battery**:
- Ensure you're using the latest version
- Close the app when not in use
- Check if background app refresh is enabled (Settings > General > Background App Refresh)

### Photo/Media Issues

**Photos not uploading**:
1. Grant camera and photo library permissions
2. Check file size (reduce if over 10MB)
3. Verify stable internet connection

**Can't take photos in-app**:
1. Go to device Settings
2. Find Japan Lean Experience app
3. Enable Camera permission
4. Restart the app
